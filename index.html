Biography
I am a PhD student specializing in Affective Computing at the AIWELL Lab, which is associated with the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC). My academic journey began at the Universitat de Barcelona (UB), where I completed my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. Subsequently, I pursued a Master's Degree in Computer Vision from the Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona (UAB). Currently, during my PhD, I am focused on decoding human behavior by leveraging multimodal and contextual data, focusing on explainability aspects.
