About Me

PhD student in computer vision, affective computing, and social robotics.

I am a PhD student at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC) in Barcelona, working at the intersection of computer vision, affective computing, and social robotics.

My research explores how intelligent systems can perceive and respond to human affect in real-world human–robot interaction scenarios. I collaborate closely with researchers at MIT and Northeastern University, with applications spanning education, healthcare, and everyday human–robot collaboration.
